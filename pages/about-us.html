Our Story

Naivik started as a visionary venture in 2014 when our founders noticed how many enterprise organizations were slow to adopt new technologies like automation, cloud computing, and AI.

It’s clear that firms not using these technologies will be left in the dust. 73% of IT leaders say automation improves efficiency by 50% and reduces overhead by up to 50%. Global firms are already using AI to strengthen functions like customer service, cybersecurity, and inventory management.

However, it hasn’t always been easy to get started. A Deloitte study found over 55% of companies state lack of experience as a barrier toward implementing end-to-end automation. Many business leaders implement automation and AI without a clear strategy of where to use it in their organization.
